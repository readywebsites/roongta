Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying property with us.

What documents are required for booking?

You will need your PAN card, Aadhar card, passport-sized photographs, and a booking amount cheque. For loans, additional income proof documents will be required.

Do you provide assistance with home loans?

Yes, we have tie-ups with all major nationalized and private banks to help you get the best home loan rates and quick processing.

Is there a cancellation policy?

Yes, cancellation policies vary by project stage. Please refer to the booking agreement or contact our sales team for specific details regarding deductions and refunds.

Are your projects RERA registered?

Absolutely. All our ongoing and new projects are registered with RERA Gujarat, ensuring complete transparency and adherence to regulations.
